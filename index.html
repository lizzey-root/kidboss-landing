What is Kidboss?

Where Storytelling Meets
the Snack Aisle

We're building a content series and a CPG brand in parallel — uniting entertainment with a physical product you can actually experience. Great storytelling and high-quality content will shape the future of consumer brands, and we're building at the forefront.

🎬

50 Original Episodes

An 11-minute episodic series on YouTube. Familiar format, rotating chaos, deeply rewatchable. Mystery, reality, action and parody — all from the boardroom.

🧪

A Product You Can Taste

Low sugar, clean label, allergen-free flavoured milks, syrups, and toppings. TikTok-ready, colourful, mixable — where food meets a science experiment.

🌍

A World You Can Enter

Kidboss HQ is a modern Willy Wonka factory — trampoline floors, candyfloss lampshades, bouncy ball chairs. An office where imagination runs the show.

What We Stand For

Built on Values, Not Just Vibes

🛡️

Protect Childhood

We build content that nourishes — emotionally, creatively, culturally. Viewers should leave every moment feeling fed, not marketed to.

✨

Champion Imagination

Creativity isn't chaos without consequences. It's a tool for connection, self-expression and building futures kids may never otherwise see.

🛋️

Family Permission

We give families permission to enjoy time together — on the sofa, at the table, and yes, even playing with their food.

🧠

Subconscious Learning

Lessons are never taught — they're discovered through storytelling. Learning to apologise, listen, lead and fail without shame.
